New developments in hard- and software make it possible to reach online customers far beyond their desktop computers and blur the boundaries between online and "real" life. Emerging devices enable new forms of human-machine interaction and redefine the way in which online retail is done. ecomhack.io 2015 is all about mobile technology, emerging devices, keyboard-free interaction, a natural shopping experience as an integral part of our everyday lives and the role of APIs in shaping the future of e-commerce.

What is all that about?

The concept

On this weekend in Berlin, we bring together some of the brightest minds in the commerce space to enter a special competition

  • Innovative companies will send their developer experts to help participants working with their API technologies and their hardware.
  • Developer teams of up to 6 people create amazing hacks based on this technology.
  • A jury of internationally renowned experts votes for the best, most inspiring and innovative apps and awards the development teams with awesome prizes.

If you are:

  • An experienced commerce developer or a creative mind.
  • Interested in new technologies and like to think out the box.

This event is perfect for you to:

  • Meet great people who are interested in working with modern API technology and recent software concepts. Get together and learn from each other!
  • Enjoy great food & drinks in an excellent location.
  • Hit the jackpot & win fantastic prices, some of which you won’t get anywhere else.

Rules & Judging Criteria

Here are the rules for the ecomhack.io competition:

  • Projects should be started from scratch at the first day of the event.
  • Use 1 or more of the provided APIs
  • Teams can consist of only 1 person but no more than 5 people.
  • Your team and your app will be judged based on 3 criteria:
    • Are you solving a real problem in an interesting way?
    • How well did you present?
    • How much progress did you make on your application?
